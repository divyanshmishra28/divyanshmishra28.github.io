Digital Timer with Alarm

The time can be determined through the seven-segment display used. The buzzer attached to it alerts the user of the time set.

4 inputs are given as a 4 bit BCD code so as to ring the buzzer at the desired nth minute, where n is between 1 and 10. As this timer can count up to 10 minutes, there can be a total of 10 combinations from 0000 to 1001 and the rest 6 combinations are considered invalid.

The BCD output of the third decade counter was compared with the BCD input given by the user to ring a buzzer and light up an LED at the time specified by the BCD input given by the user.

The comparison was done with 4 XOR gates, where one input of each XOR gate was a bit from the decade counter and the other input a bit from the user’s end. XOR gate was used as it gives output as zero when any two inputs are same. OR gate is used to combine results of all XOR gates, whose output is given to a NOT gate to decide the result.

Traffic Lights Controller

There are four traffic light signals which are to be controlled. These four signals have same priority as they all are main roads. First of all, the directions are red. When the reset mode is ON the signal of North direction is green, East direction is yellow remaining all the directions are Red. This state is particular assigned as S0. Later the controller is switched to state S1 where the East direction is green, South direction is yellow and the remaining directions are red. Next by switching the State to S2 South direction is green, West direction is yellow. Finally, during S3 state green is on in the west direction and yellow in north direction, the remaining directions are red.

LEDs and 7-segment displays are used to represent the outputs. Push buttons are used to opt for select lines as the switching of states and reset button are controlled by the user.

Tic Tac Toe

A keypad is used for user input. 9 keys would each represent a square of the tic tac toe board. An assembly language program would be implemented such as the game would alternate turns between the two players.
